Chris Jenkins

About

Computer Science PhD student working with the Computational Logic Center, matriculated 2017 Aug. Advised by Dr. Aaron Stump, and research assistantship with same developing Cedille, an experimental dependent type theory. CV.

Research interests include the design of programming languages with Curry-style type theories, verified elaboration of sophisticated surface language features to a simple core theory, dependent types, and categorical semantics of datatypes and terminating recursion schemes.

Creator of Cedille casts 1-9 on Cedille Programming Language YouTube channel.
